BEDROOM

The most intimate room in the home is the bedroom. Quilts, blankets and accessories are essential when designing a pleasant space to relax in. The bed is undoubtedly the main feature of the bedroom. Dress yours with the latest in sheets, cushions and duvet covers: designs in neutral colours, with plant prints or original geometric patterns. The right colour combination is the key to achieving a comfortable and relaxing space. Choose a delicate colour range or create an original counterpoint with lots of personality by combining different textile items such as bedroom curtains or rugs.
