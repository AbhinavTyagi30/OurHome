MODIFYING OR CANCELLING YOUR ORDER

Please remember that you can only modify or cancel your order before it reaches the final stages of preparation for dispatch.

MY WISHLIST

You can save your favourite items in your WISHLIST and always have them to hand. To use this option you must be a registered customer. You can save your favourite items in your WISHLIST by clicking the heart icon on each item. You can create different WISHLISTS and modify them. You can make your WISHLIST public and share it as a link by using the "Copy link" option. If you decide to complete the purchase, you will need to add the items to the basket and follow the usual payment process.

TICKETS AND INVOICES+
PURCHASE RECEIPT

Purchases on zarahome.com do not generate paper receipts. You will find your purchase receipt available for download in your account.

INVOICE

You can request an invoice for your order as an individual or a business. You will be able to access the invoice on your account once the order has been dispatched.

*If you have purchased as a guest, access your order history and download receipts as well as invoices.

SHIPPING AND COSTS+
HOME DELIVERY

Home delivery fees are 45 EUR.

The estimated delivery time will be between 7-10 working days.

Please bear in mind that some of our deliveries may be affected by import costs out of our control. We will let you know if these costs are included in the final amount of the purchase or need to be paid when receiving the order. If the second case applies, we suggest that you get in touch with your local Customs Office for further information.

You will receive email updates about the status of the delivery of your order and if you have purchased as a registered customer you will be able to check its status on your account too.

Returns and Refunds+
RETURN OPTIONS

These are the options for returning your items:

FROM YOUR HOME

If you have purchased as a registered customer, you should access your order information from your account and request the return. You will receive email updates about the status of the delivery of your order and if you have purchased as a registered customer you will be able to check its status on your account too.

The items you return must be in the same condition you received them in.

REFUND

The refund will be made via the same payment method used to make the purchase. If you have used more than one payment method, it will be refunded to both methods proportionally.

You will receive email confirmation of the return once it has been completed. If you have not received a refund in your account after more than 14 days, please show the confirmation to your bank so they can speed up the process.
